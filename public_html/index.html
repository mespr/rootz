The platform for corporate digital identity

The world is going Digital but the enterprise lacks a usable digital root of identity

The advent of AI enables everyone to manufacture data but with no foundations of provenance

Global trade is transitioning to digital but without a framework for Corp governance or authorization.

Personal Identity technologies are not a substitute for a solid corporate identity with roles and authorizations.

The old model of certificates has proven hard to manage and needs a global upgrade

The solution

Rootz provides a new Identity model rooted in the existing framework of global corporate registrars.

Rootz provides the technology and process for manageable custody and control by the Board of directors and senior management.

Rootz provides the operational models to use blockchain, Apps, Dapps and process to enable simple use cases for standard business process.

Roots offers relying parties the validation technology to assure proof of compliance and the digital forensics needed for reliance.

A Utility Business Model

Services and consulting to establish an enterprise root of identity and policy

Transaction and licensing built on a software paradigm of use for single use identities, administration, and operations.

Validation software for any relying parties (customers, regulators, partners, suppliers)

Transactions enable with classic e-commerce, subscription and the use of tokens and blockchain.
